What Is GEO?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of making your website visible to AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. As millions of users shift to AI for answers, appearing in these results is the new frontier of search visibility.
Why Schema Markup Matters
Schema markup is structured data that helps search engines and AI models understand your content. Sites without it are virtually invisible to AI search engines. Our audit checks for FAQPage, LocalBusiness, Article, and Product schema — the highest-impact GEO actions you can take.
Core Web Vitals & Page Speed
Google uses Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS) as direct ranking signals. A slow page doesn't just frustrate visitors — it actively hurts your position in search results. Our audit flags performance bottlenecks and gives you a clear performance score.
Downloadable PDF Report
Every audit generates a clean, professional PDF report you can download and share with clients or your team. It includes your SEO score, GEO analysis, critical issues, and a numbered list of recommended fixes — ready to present.
