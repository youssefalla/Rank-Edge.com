What is GEO and why does it matter?
▾
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is about making your website visible to AI-powered search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Microsoft Copilot. As millions of users switch to AI for answers, ranking there is becoming as important as ranking on Google.

Do Pro and Agency plans include white-label reports?
▾
White-label PDF reports — with your agency's logo and branding — are available on the Agency plan. Pro plans include priority-branded RankEdge reports.

Switching to annual billing gives you 25% off — billed as one payment upfront. Pro goes from $19/mo to $14/mo, and Agency from $79/mo to $59/mo.
